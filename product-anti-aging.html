About the Anti-Aging Formula

As skin matures, cell turnover slows, collagen synthesis declines, and the epidermis thins. The Anti-Aging formula addresses all three simultaneously: AHAs restore turnover rate to a more youthful pace; Retinol Booster signals fibroblasts to ramp up collagen production; and a Tripeptide Complex reinforces structural integrity at the dermal level.

The result is skin that doesn't just look exfoliated — it looks renewed. Clinical participant assessment showed a 32% reduction in the appearance of fine lines and a 28% improvement in perceived firmness after 8 weeks of consistent use.

  • 32% reduction in visible fine lines (8-week clinical assessment)
  • 28% improvement in perceived skin firmness
  • Stimulates collagen synthesis alongside surface exfoliation
  • Addresses the three primary mechanisms of skin ageing in one treatment

Key Benefits

  • Encapsulated Retinol (0.1%): Time-released micro-encapsulation delivers retinol slowly to maximise efficacy and dramatically reduce the irritation typical of retinol products.
  • Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1 & -7: A signal peptide duo that communicates with fibroblasts to upregulate collagen and elastin synthesis.
  • Glycolic Acid (9%): At slightly higher concentration than the Classic formula, Glycolic penetrates more deeply to address texture irregularities that deepen with age.
  • Adenosine: Clinically proven to reduce the depth of fine lines by firming the dermis — one of the few cosmetic ingredients with rigorous peer-reviewed evidence.
  • Hyaluronic Acid (3 weights): Triple-molecular-weight Hyaluronic Acid hydrates at surface, mid, and deep dermal levels — essential for mature skin that loses moisture rapidly.
1

Evening use only. Due to the Retinol Booster, use this formula exclusively in your evening routine. Never apply before sun exposure. Build frequency slowly — begin once weekly before progressing to twice weekly.

2

Cleanse with a cream cleanser. For mature skin, a cream or oil cleanser preserves precious moisture. Ensure skin is completely dry before application.

3

Apply avoiding the eye contour. Fine lines around the eyes require a dedicated eye-area retinol product — never apply this formula there. Stay on forehead, cheeks, chin, and neck.

4

Full 10-minute contact. Mature skin benefits from the full contact time. The encapsulated retinol requires the AHAs to have opened the surface channels before releasing effectively.

5

Seal with a rich night cream. After rinsing, apply a nourishing night cream immediately. The Tripeptide Complex works best when the skin barrier is sealed overnight. Always SPF during the day.
