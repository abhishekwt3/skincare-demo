About the Sensitive Formula

The Sensitive formula was developed in direct response to customer feedback from those with rosacea, eczema-prone skin, and general reactivity who felt excluded from chemical exfoliation. By reducing overall acid concentration, lowering the Glycolic Acid fraction in favour of the gentler Lactic Acid, and fortifying the formula with anti-inflammatory botanicals, we created a peel that works without triggering flares.

Clinical testing showed zero instances of acute irritation across a panel of 80 self-declared sensitive skin participants, with 94% reporting improved texture after 4 weeks of twice-weekly use.

  • Clinically tested — zero acute irritation in 80-person panel
  • Suitable for rosacea-prone and eczema-adjacent skin
  • Lactic-acid-dominant for gentle exfoliation with moisture retention
  • Supports barrier integrity rather than compromising it

Key Benefits

  • Centella Asiatica (CICA): One of dermatology's most respected botanicals for calming inflammation, repairing barrier function, and promoting healing.
  • Oat Extract (Avena Sativa): Beta-glucan from oats forms a soothing, moisture-locking film that reduces reactivity and redness.
  • Lactic Acid (8%): Gentler than Glycolic, Lactic exfoliates at a larger molecular size — staying closer to the surface for controlled, comfortable renewal.
  • Bisabolol: The calming compound from chamomile; reduces redness and sensitisation post-exfoliation.
  • Ceramide Complex: Replenishes the lipid layer essential for barrier health — rarely found in peeling solutions at this concentration.
1

Patch test first. Sensitive-skin users should perform a 24-hour patch test on the inner arm before first facial use. Apply a small amount, wait, and assess for any reaction.

2

Start with 5 minutes. For first-time use, leave on for only 5 minutes. Gradually build to the full 10 minutes over 2–3 sessions as your skin acclimates.

3

Apply to dry, intact skin. Avoid applying over broken skin, active eczema patches, or open rosacea pustules. Use on calm, healthy skin only.

4

Rinse with cool water. Cool water helps close pores and soothes any post-peel warmth. Avoid hot water immediately after rinsing.

5

Barrier-first aftercare. Follow with a ceramide-rich moisturiser — the Ceramide Complex in the formula prepares skin for enhanced absorption. Always finish with SPF during daylight hours.
