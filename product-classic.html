About the Classic Formula

Designed as the entry point into professional-grade chemical exfoliation, the ABP-11% Classic Peeling Solution is suitable for all skin types — including those new to acids. The formulation layers Glycolic Acid, Lactic Acid, and Tartaric Acid in a pH-optimised base that maximises efficacy while staying well within safe tolerance thresholds.

Unlike physical scrubs that cause microtears, this leave-on solution dissolves intercellular "glue" to accelerate natural cell turnover — revealing brighter, softer, more even-toned skin after just one use.

  • Visibly reduces surface texture and congestion
  • Minimises the look of enlarged pores
  • Improves skin clarity and luminosity within 2–4 weeks
  • pH-balanced for maximum safety and absorption
  • Compatible with most routine products when spaced correctly

Key Benefits

  • Surface Renewal: Glycolic Acid (smallest AHA molecule) penetrates readily to loosen dead cell build-up, smoothing texture and boosting radiance.
  • Deep Hydration: Lactic Acid exfoliates while simultaneously drawing moisture into the skin — rare in a peeling solution.
  • Pore Refinement: BHA (Salicylic Acid 1%) is oil-soluble, allowing it to travel inside pores to clear sebum blockages.
  • Antioxidant Protection: Tartaric Acid stabilises the formula and adds a free-radical defence layer.
  • Gentle on Barrier: Low-irritation formula with a skin-safe pH keeps the moisture barrier intact, preventing over-exfoliation.
1

Cleanse thoroughly. Begin with a gentle, non-stripping cleanser to remove makeup, SPF, and excess oil. Pat skin completely dry — moisture on the skin will dilute the formula and reduce efficacy.

2

Apply evenly. Using fingertips or a fan brush, apply a thin, even layer of the ABP-11% Classic Peeling Solution across face, neck, or décolletage. Avoid the eye contour, nostrils, and lips.

3

Leave on for 10 minutes. A mild tingling sensation is normal. Do not exceed the recommended contact time. If stinging intensifies, rinse immediately. Never use with occlusive masks or wet skin.

4

Rinse thoroughly. Remove with cool water, ensuring no residue remains around hairline or jawline. Follow immediately with a hydrating toner or essence.

5

Moisturise and protect. Apply your regular moisturiser and — critically — SPF 30+ during the day. AHAs increase photosensitivity; daily sunscreen is non-negotiable. Use 2–3× weekly for best results.
