About the Brightening Formula

Developed in response to the growing demand for a peeling solution that addresses pigmentation at its source, ABP-11% Brightening is a dual-action treatment. The AHA/BHA base renews the surface while brightening agents interrupt the melanin pathway — delivering a more even, luminous complexion that improves with every use.

The formula is particularly effective for Fitzpatrick skin types III–VI, where post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH) following breakouts or sun exposure is most persistent.

  • Fades dark spots and post-acne marks within 4–6 weeks
  • Inhibits melanin production to prevent new spots forming
  • Brightens overall complexion and boosts radiance
  • Compatible with Vitamin C serums (use separately, different sessions)

Key Benefits

  • Vitamin C Derivative (Ascorbyl Glucoside): A stable, non-irritating form of Vitamin C that brightens existing spots and intercepts new melanin formation.
  • Kojic Acid: Derived from fermented rice, it gently inhibits tyrosinase — the enzyme responsible for dark spot formation.
  • Glycolic Acid (8%): Clears pigmented dead cells from the surface to rapidly reveal clearer, more uniform skin underneath.
  • Niacinamide Booster: Reduces inflammation that triggers post-blemish darkening, breaking the breakout-to-dark-spot cycle.
  • Ferulic Acid: Stabilises Vitamin C activity and provides additional antioxidant protection against UV-induced pigmentation.
1

Cleanse and tone. Start with a gentle cleanser, then apply an alcohol-free toner to ensure your pH is balanced before the peeling solution. Dry skin thoroughly.

2

Apply to target areas. For hyperpigmentation, concentrate application on affected zones — cheeks, forehead, or chin. Can be applied all-over for general brightening. Avoid the delicate eye contour.

3

10-minute contact time. Leave on for precisely 10 minutes. The Vitamin C and Kojic Acid are active throughout the full contact period. Do not rinse early unless experiencing discomfort.

4

Rinse and apply Vitamin C serum. After rinsing, the freshly exfoliated surface is primed for optimal serum absorption. Apply a Vitamin C serum at this stage for compounded brightening.

5

SPF is essential. AHAs increase photosensitivity by up to 50%. Daily SPF 30–50 use is critical, especially when treating pigmentation — UV exposure without protection will darken spots further.
