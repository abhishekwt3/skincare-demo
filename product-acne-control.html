About the Acne Control Formula

Acne-prone skin faces a compounding problem: oil overproduction clogs pores, trapped sebum triggers breakouts, breakouts leave dark marks, and the cycle continues. ABP-11% Acne Control is designed to interrupt this cycle at every stage.

The elevated BHA (Salicylic Acid at 2%) penetrates deep into sebaceous follicles where standard AHAs cannot reach. Zinc PCA simultaneously regulates oil production at the source, while Niacinamide addresses the post-inflammatory redness that precedes dark marks. Surface-level AHAs ensure dead-cell turnover doesn't slow the clearing process.

  • Unclogs pores with oil-soluble BHA at effective 2% concentration
  • Regulates sebum production with Zinc PCA
  • Fades post-breakout marks with Niacinamide and AHAs
  • Non-comedogenic, tested on acne-prone skin

Key Benefits

  • Salicylic Acid (2%): Maximum OTC concentration for oil-soluble pore-clearing — dissolves the plug of dead cells and sebum that causes blackheads and whiteheads.
  • Zinc PCA: Clinically proven to reduce sebum production when used consistently — oiliness decreases noticeably within 4 weeks.
  • Niacinamide (4%): Interrupts melanin transfer that turns red post-acne spots into dark marks. Also reduces pore appearance and strengthens barrier.
  • Tea Tree Extract: Naturally antimicrobial; reduces the presence of C. acnes bacteria on the skin surface without harsh stripping.
  • Glycolic Acid (8%): Clears the surface layer of dead cells that trap oil and sebum, allowing BHA to penetrate more effectively.
1

Double cleanse if wearing SPF or makeup. Oil build-up creates a barrier. A double cleanse (oil cleanser + gel cleanser) ensures the Salicylic Acid can penetrate pores rather than sitting on residue.

2

Apply to full face or target zones. Can be used all-over for oily, acne-prone skin, or spot-applied to congested zones (T-zone, chin, jawline). Avoid active open breakouts — Salicylic is more effective on closed comedones.

3

10-minute contact — no more. Salicylic Acid at 2% is highly effective and does not need extended contact time. Never exceed 10 minutes; this can trigger dryness and paradoxically increase oil production.

4

Rinse thoroughly, especially jawline. Salicylic residue on the jawline and neck can cause dryness. Rinse for at least 30 seconds with cool water.

5

Lightweight moisturiser and SPF. Acne-prone skin often skips moisturiser — a critical mistake after exfoliation. Use a non-comedogenic gel moisturiser and SPF 30+ every morning. Unprotected post-peel skin is significantly more susceptible to UV-triggered breakouts.
